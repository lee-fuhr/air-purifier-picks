Indoor air quality has become a central concern for many households, and a good air purifier can make a real difference in reducing allergens, dust, pet dander, and even odors. Many top performers now include activated carbon layers for VOCs and offers app connectivity, but a simple no‑frills unit with a great filter is often all you need.
